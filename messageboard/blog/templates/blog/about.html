About this app

Overview

This application was developed by Patrick Davis as a dissertation project during his final year at uni (2019-2020).

It is designed to be a place where mangement can upload newly created/updated rota images, company announcements and pretty much anything else. Staff members can log in at any time to view this information.

Staff can also comment on posts and ask questions and also raise possible shift swap queries.

To contact the author of this web app, please send an email.

User Roles

Users can: Log in, view posts, edit their profile.

Superusers (management) can: Manage users, create posts, access the admin panel and the above.
